Meatlovers Pizza

If you're a carnivore, then this is the pizza for you

Just make sure you aren't around any vegetarians when you're eating this.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce or barbeque sauce
  • MEAT
    • Bacon
    • Sausage
    • Ham
    • Pepperoni
    • Chicken
  • Mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Spread your choice of sauce evenly over base
  2. Top base with your selection of meat (be generous)
  3. Sprinkle cheese on top, then add swirl of barbeque sauce
  4. Bake pizza in the oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is melted
  5. Cut into even slices and enjoy!
