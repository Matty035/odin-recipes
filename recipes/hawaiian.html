Hawaiian pizza

Yes, pineapple really does belong on pizza

And no the pizza was not created in Hawaii as the name suggests, it was actually created in Canada of all places.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce or bbq sauce
  • Pineapple
  • Bacon
  • Mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Spread sauce evenly over base
  2. Top base with bacon and lots of pineapple
  3. Bake pizza in the oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is melted
  4. Cut into even slices and enjoy!
