Pepperoni Pizza

The most simple but tasty pizza

A famous classic, the pepperoni is everyones favourite. The hardest part is to being able to resist the urge to keep eating.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni (LOTS OF IT)
  • Mozzaella Cheese
  • Any other toppings of your choice (Not required for the classic pepperoni)

Directions

  1. Spread pizza sauce evenly over base
  2. Top base with pepperoni slices, then sprinkle cheese on top
  3. Bake pizza in the oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is melted
  4. Cut into even slices and enjoy!
